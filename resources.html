Emergency Crisis Resources

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741
  • The Trevor Project: 1-866-488-7386

Campus Support Services

  • Your University Counseling Center: Check your campus website for contact information
  • Peer Support Groups: Available through the student union or counseling services

Mental Health Apps

  • Headspace: Meditation and mindfulness
  • Calm: Sleep, meditation, and relaxation
  • Woebot: AI-driven mental health support

Grief Organizations

  • GriefShare: Support groups for those grieving
  • The Dougy Center: Support for grieving children

Academic Support

  • Study Groups: Organize or join study groups for your courses
  • Tutoring Services: Available through your university

Self-Care Tips for Grieving College Students

  • Take time for yourself, and allow yourself to grieve.
  • Reach out to friends, family, or counselors for support.
  • Engage in activities you enjoy, even if you don’t feel like it at first.
  • Establish a routine to provide a sense of normalcy.
  • Consider journaling to express your feelings.
