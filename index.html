About This Project

Grief is an emotional experience that significantly affects mental health, academic performance, and personal wellbeing. College students facing the loss of a loved one encounter unique challenges while managing academics, new environments, and the weight of their grief.

This AI chatbot provides a low-cost, accessible, and consistent resource for emotional support during your grieving process.

Key Features

🎤

Voice & Text Chat

Choose between voice-based or text conversations. Voice interactions provide emotional tone recognition for better support.

💾

Long-Term Memory

Your chatbot remembers past conversations, allowing for continuity and personalized support over time.

📅

Personalization

Track important dates, mood patterns, and academic stressors. Receive gentle reminders and support on significant occasions.

📔

Optional Journaling

Express yourself through text or voice journaling. Your entries remain private and securely stored.

🎓

Academic Support

Gentle check-ins about coursework, exams, and deadlines with coping suggestions tailored to grieving students.

🔒

Privacy & Safety

Anonymous usage available. Crisis detection with immediate redirection to emergency resources (988 hotline).

Emergency Resources

Campus Counseling

Contact your college's counseling center for professional support and grief support groups
